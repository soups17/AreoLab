▮ 4 · SECTION — HESS–SMITH PANEL METHOD

The airfoil contour is rebuilt from m, p, t above and re-solved here in your browser: 112 panels, constant source strength per panel plus one shared vortex strength, flow tangency at each midpoint, and a Kutta condition at the trailing edge.
